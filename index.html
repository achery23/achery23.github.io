Black and Caribbean Women Writers

Some writers and texts that have touched me personally include Toni Morrison's Beloved; Gayl Jones' Corregidora; Paule Marshall's Brown Girl, Brownstones; Jamaica Kincaid's Annie John; Jesmyn Ward's Sing, Unburied, Sing; Edwidge Danticat's Breath, Eyes, Memory; and Patricia Powell's The Pagoda.
